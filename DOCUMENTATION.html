SMART CRADLE

INTRODUCTION

A smart cradle is a type of baby cradle that integrates technology to enhance the safety, comfort, and convenience of both the baby and the parent. This innovative product has gained significant attention and popularity in recent years, with many parents embracing the idea of using technology to assist in the care of their babies.The concept of a smart cradle was introduced as a solution to common challenges that parents face when caring for newborns. These challenges include sleepless nights, crying babies, and the constant monitoring of the baby's condition. In conclusion, smart cradles are an innovative and practical solution for parents who want to take advantage of technology to enhance their baby's safety and comfort. With features such as motion sensors, temperature control, music and sound, and monitoring and alert systems, smart cradles provide a host of benefits for both the baby and the parent. While it's important to remember that no technology can replace the watchful eye and attentive care of a parent or caregiver, smart cradles can be an invaluable tool to help parents care for their babies more effectively and efficiently.

ABSTRACT

As we are very well familiar with the hurdles faced by Parents to nurture their infant and especially in case if both the Parents are working. To give 24 hours of time in such cases is next to impossible. However they still need to look after their babies, thereby increasing workload and stress. They either send their babies to their parents or hire a baby caregiver while they are working. Some parents worry about the safety of their babies in the care of others. Thus they go home to check on their babies during their free time, such as lunch or tea break . A baby Monitoring System that can monitor the babies' condition real time is proposed to solve these problems. A baby monitoring System consisting of a camera and microphone without limitations of coverage. The proposed solution involves live monitoring of the child. Sound Sensor for the detection of the child's crying activity. When detect crying, plays a song and starts swinging. The Thermal sensor notifies the parent about the environmental temperature near the baby and switch on the fan automatically according to the temperature. Thus, we need to develop something unique that can help Parents to have a continuous watch on the Baby and can notify about the same. Thus, we have come up with an idea to design a standalone application I e., Cradle System, which will help the Parents to monitor their child even if they are busy for particular time & detect every activity of the baby. This system considers all the minute details required for the care & protection of the Baby in the cradle. The baby monitoring system is attached to the cradle so that an incubator kind of environment will be created for the baby. The design of smartness & innovation comes with the use of technologies/methodologies which include Modules like Raspberry Pi, Arduino, moisture & Temperature sensing, Swing Automation, Cry Detecting Mechanism, servo motors, etc. each sensors has their own functionality ,these sensors are connected to the endpoints like Arduino . The data generated by the sensors is given to the processing unit, the processing unit acts up on the r espective information received , so that the values which were generated by the endpoints can be monitored by the serial monitor. If the recorded readings show any abnormalities, the necessary actions like controlling temperature, switching on or off the fan, setting up cradle's movement, playing music for the baby will be taken. The baby monitoring system prototype helps the parents in time management and makes it easier for the caretakers as well. This baby monitoring system is proven to have less harm for the baby with the most accuracy. This monitoring system is a highly efficient for real time monitoring with the best security measures.it is very useful for busy people. What will happen if we use IOT: cry detection mechanism which detects cry of the baby and send instant mobile app notifications to theuser Turn on and off functions of the mini fan can be fully controled using the mobile application also according to the need of the user. Swing of the cradle is fully controlled by the Mobile application , a cloud server is maintained to store database and send notifictions .
